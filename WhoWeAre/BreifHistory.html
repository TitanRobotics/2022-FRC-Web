A Brief History of Titan Robotics

General Background

We are an entirely student lead, student designed team.
We receive large amounts of support from Caterpillar, and have the assistance of a few of their engineers.

2007

Team started: created by a small group of individuals who believed that the STEM education at our school was inadequate.
Won highest rookie seed at Midwest Regional
Began planting FIRST seeds at Oswego High School.
Team size~10

2008

Assisted Gear it Forward, team 2338 in beginning with help from Caterpillar.
Competed in Midwest regional.
Began planting FIRST seeds in Batavia High School
Team size~20

2009

Assisted in creating Method 2 Madness, team 2741 with help from Caterpillar.
Competed in Midwest Regional and Wisconsin Regional, placing in the semifinals for each.
Began incorporating more advanced physics into our robots with our custom drivetrain designed to take advantage of a low friction floor.
Began planting seeds at West Aurora High School.
Team Size~40

2010

Public showing of Lunacy in Rockford with the help of team 1625, Winnovation.
Team 2741 split due to different philosophies, however they still have a FRC team.
Club showing to West Aurora High School, invited the students to join our team.
Competing in Midwest Regional and Minnesota 10000 Lakes.
Team Size~50
